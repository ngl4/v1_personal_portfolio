Introduction
I am Binh (Cindy) Lam, a Third-Culture Kid, born in Vietnam and ethnically Chinese. I want to become part of the team as a Front-End Web Developer. I have been growing up with the emerging web development industry and have always been interested in it.

In the past year, before I graduated from the University of Pittsburgh with a bachelor’s degree in Communication, I was grateful to contribute back to the Pitt community by taking on two jobs simultaneously. I was a media consultant at the Language Media Center and a Resident Assistant at the Residence Hall.

I enjoy not only direct communication between people, but also visual communication that influences on different users and audiences. What attracts me to the Front-End Developer role is the ability to bridge the connection between clients’ needs and users' wants.

In order to advance my coding skills, I attend UPENN LPS Coding Bootcamp. This opportunity allows me to showcase my passion in coding and strengthened all the Front-End coding languages, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, various CSS libraries, JavaScript, jQuery, some Back-End coding using APIs and Node.js. In the meantime, I have built up some professional portfolios.
